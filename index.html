Full Transparency About How We Operate.

Transparency.bio exists to explain – openly and clearly – how ronarn AS runs its network of independent, affiliate-supported comparison and education websites.

  • How affiliate partnerships influence our content (they don’t).
  • How rankings and comparisons are created.
  • How we remain fully independent from brands.

1. Editorial Independence

All reviews, rankings and educational articles across the ronarn network (including Retynol.com, Niacinamide.bio, AcneHeals.com and others) are written independently of any commercial influence.

No brand can buy a ranking, placement or recommendation.

2. Affiliate Relationships

Some pages include affiliate links to retailers such as Amazon, Dermstore and official brand sites. If you click these links and make a purchase, ronarn AS may earn a small commission.

This does **not** affect price, ranking or editorial decisions.

3. Ranking Methodology

Rankings are based on product formulation, ingredient science, real-user experience patterns and value relative to price – not payments, sponsorships or brand deals.

4. Data Transparency

Transparency.bio does not collect personal data. Other sites in the ronarn network may use privacy-friendly analytics.

5. Who Owns This Network?

The entire comparison network is owned and operated by ronarn AS in Norway.
