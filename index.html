1. Mission & Transparency Philosophy

ronarn AS operates a network of independent education and comparison sites. We prioritise accuracy, clarity, and user trust over monetisation.

  • No brand pays for inclusion or placement.
  • No sponsored rankings, advertorials or paid “trusted reviews”.
  • Affiliate links never influence product scoring.

2. Ranking Transparency – How Products Are Evaluated

Rankings are based on **editorial criteria** that do not change from brand to brand. These include:

  • Ingredient analysis
  • Formula strength & suitability
  • Safety considerations
  • User feedback patterns
  • Price-to-size value

If two products pay different affiliate commissions, rankings DO NOT change.

3. Affiliate Links – How They Work

Many comparison hubs (Retynol.com, Hylaronic.com, Niacinimide.com etc.) use affiliate links where available.

Our rules:

  • No affiliate link = product can still rank #1.
  • Affiliate income never affects editorial scoring.
  • We disclose affiliate links on every page that uses them.
  • We only link to retailers that are widely trusted (Amazon, official brands, etc.).

Detailed affiliate disclosures live at Disclosure.bio.

4. Integrity Controls & Editorial Guardrails

To maintain trust, we have clear checks that prevent commercial interference:

  • All rankings are reviewed before publication.
  • All medical statements require evidence-based support.
  • No brand can request removal or ranking changes.
  • Corrections are logged on the page level where relevant.
  • Content updates are versioned by revision date.

Editorial standards live at Standards.bio.

5. AI Usage – How AI Helps (and Where It Doesn’t)

AI is used as a drafting and research assistant. All AI-assisted content is reviewed, corrected and approved by a human before publication.

  • AI never decides rankings.
  • AI does not write medical advice.
  • AI drafts are edited to match human tone and accuracy.
  • High-risk claims require manual fact-checking.
